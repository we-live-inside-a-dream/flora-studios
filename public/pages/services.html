Our Interior Design Process in Toronto

1

Initial Consultation

We begin with a detailed discussion of your vision, requirements, and budget. This helps us understand your needs and establish project parameters for your Toronto interior design project.

2

Concept Development

Our team creates initial design concepts, incorporating your ideas with our expertise as a Toronto interior designer to develop preliminary sketches and 3D visualizations.

3

Detailed Design

We refine the chosen concept into detailed interior design plans, including technical specifications and material selections for your Toronto space.

4

Planning & Permits

We handle all necessary documentation and applications for planning permissions and building permits required for your Toronto interior design project.

5

Construction Supervision

Throughout the construction phase, we provide regular site visits and supervision to ensure your Toronto interior design project is executed according to specifications.
