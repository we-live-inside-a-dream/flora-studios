Our Story

Flora Studio was founded by Flora Naeim with the vision of transforming spaces into beautiful, functional environments. As a leading Toronto interior designer, our team works closely with clients across Toronto and the Greater Toronto Area to bring their ideas to life, ensuring every project is tailored to their unique needs and preferences.

With a commitment to excellence and a passion for design, Flora Naeim has established Flora Studio as a leader in Toronto interior design. We believe in the power of collaboration and strive to create spaces that inspire and delight. Our approach combines modern aesthetics with timeless principles, resulting in interiors that are both contemporary and enduring.
